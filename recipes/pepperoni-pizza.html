Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Pepperoni pizza is the classic and go to flavor for pizzas.

Who doesn't remember being in a friend's house playing videogames while enjoying a good 'ol pepperoni pizza.

Ingredients

  • ½ 12 ounce can Tomato Paste
  • 1 teaspoon crushed oregano
  • 1 teaspoon crushed basil
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
